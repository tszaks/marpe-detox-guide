MARPE DETOX - APPROVED FOODS
=============================

DAYS 1-10 (Plant-Based Phase)
-----------------------------
VEGETABLES: All leafy greens, cruciferous (broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage),
root veggies (beets, carrots, sweet potatoes), squash, peppers, tomatoes,
celery, cucumbers, asparagus, artichokes, garlic, onions, ginger

PROTEINS: Beans, lentils, flaxseed (NO meat/seafood)

FATS: Avocado oil, coconut oil, olive oil, avocados, organic butter

BEVERAGES: Teas, bone broth

FRUITS: Berries only


DAYS 11-18+ (Full Protein Phase)
--------------------------------
Everything above PLUS:
- Meat: Steak, beef, chicken, turkey
- Seafood: only wild caught fish, no shellfish


AVOID (Entire Program)
----------------------
Dairy, almond milk, caffeine, sugar, grains, potatoes, sweet fruits,
canned foods, eggs, mushrooms, gum, pork, alcohol, nuts, shellfish, soybeans or soy products, not canned products, avoid nuts
